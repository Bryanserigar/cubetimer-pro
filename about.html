How to Use CubeTimer Pro

Get started with professional speedcubing timing

1

Generate a Scramble

Click "New Scramble" to generate a WCA-compliant scramble algorithm. Each scramble is randomly generated to ensure fair and unbiased cube states.

Example: R U R' U' F2 D L2 B' R U2 F D' R2 U' B2 L F' D2 R U' L2 B'
2

Start the Inspection

Press the spacebar or click "Start Timer" to begin the 15-second inspection period. Use this time to examine the scramble and plan your solution.

üí° Tip: Practice inspection to improve your competition readiness!
3

Begin Solving

After inspection, press spacebar or click "Start Solving" to begin timing your solve. The timer will start immediately and track your time with millisecond precision.

4

Complete and Record

When you finish solving, press spacebar or click "Stop Timer" to record your time. Your solve will be automatically saved and added to your statistics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CubeTimer Pro

How accurate is the timer?

+

CubeTimer Pro uses high-precision timing with millisecond accuracy (0.001 seconds). The timer is implemented using the Web Performance API for the most accurate timing possible in a web browser.

Are the scrambles truly random?

+

Yes! All scrambles are generated using algorithms that comply with WCA (World Cube Association) standards. Each scramble is randomly generated and requires at least 4 moves to solve, ensuring fair and unbiased cube states.

Can I use this for official competitions?

+

While CubeTimer Pro uses WCA-compliant scrambles and timing methods, it should not be used for official competitions. Official WCA competitions use specific equipment and procedures. However, it's perfect for practice and unofficial competitions.

How do I export my solve data?

+

Go to the Statistics page and click the "Export Data" button. Your solve history will be downloaded as a CSV file that you can open in Excel or any spreadsheet application for further analysis.
